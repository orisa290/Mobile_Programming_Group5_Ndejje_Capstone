# Problem Analysis

## Current Situation

Students at Ndejje University lose items daily with no centralized system to report or recover them.

## Current Methods (Inefficient)

- WhatsApp group messages (scattered, hard to track)
- Physical notice boards (limited reach)
- Word of mouth (unreliable)

## Problems Identified

1. Low recovery rates (estimated less than 30%)
2. Student frustration and wasted time
3. Financial burden to replace lost items
4. No way to notify finders
5. No ownership verification

## Proposed Solution

Lost & Found Hub mobile app that allows:
- Students to report lost/found items
- Upload images of items
- Chat with finders/owners
- Track status of reports

## Expected Impact

- Increase recovery rate to 70%+
- Reduce student frustration
- Save students money
- Create centralized system
